Welcome to Stock Manager

The concept behind how the stock market works is pretty simple. Operating much like an auction house, the stock market enables buyers and sellers to negotiate prices and make trades.

An image showing stocks, value and profit/loss

Difference between Bull and Bear Markets

Every stock values opens the market with an open price and closes the market with close price. In between these 2 phases, the stock goes through many ups and downs. This application helps you to choose a stock of your choice and a date. The application then uses this information to output the stock prices for the selected stock for the selected date with open, close, highest and lowest prices.
